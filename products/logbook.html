AeroEdge Logbook

A digital logbook that actually understands pilot pain points.

The Logbook Problem

Current digital logbooks are built by software developers, not pilots.

"I have 2,000 military hours. Converting them will take weeks."

NATOPS and SHARP formats don't match FAA categories. Manual conversion is tedious and error-prone. Nobody has solved this well.

"I found a flight from 10 years ago. The app won't let me add it."

Most digital logbooks force chronological entry. Real pilots find old flights, fix errors, and enter things out of order. The software should handle it.

"I want to go digital but I have 40 years of paper logs."

Typing thousands of entries is a non-starter. OCR helps, but only if you can review and correct what it interprets. You need control.

"Am I current? I think my night currency lapsed..."

Manual currency tracking is error-prone. You shouldn't discover you're not current when you're prefighting for a night flight.
